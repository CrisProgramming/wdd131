Elizabeth Transformational Coaching

A blueprint for the website project

Site Name

Name: ElizabethTransformationalCoaching

Reason: Represents a personal brand focusing on emotional healing and transformation for individuals seeking support with inner challenges.

Site Purpose

The website aims to attract new clients and help them overcome emotional and mental struggles through personalized therapy sessions. It provides information about services and guidance on contacting the coach for help.

Scenarios

  • How can I contact the coach to start therapy sessions?
  • What services are offered, and how can they address my specific traumas and fears?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Light Blue (#ADD8E6) – Headings and buttons.

Secondary Color: Soft Lavender (#E6E6FA) – Background and accents.

Typography

  • Lora: Used for headings – Elegant and calming.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text – Readable and approachable.
